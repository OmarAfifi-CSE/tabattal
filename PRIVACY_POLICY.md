# Privacy Policy for Tabattal (تبتّل)

**Effective Date: July 2026**

Welcome to **Tabattal** (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains my practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use my App.

Tabattal was built as a passion project and a *Sadaqa Jariya* (continuous charity). My core principle is absolute privacy and an uninterrupted, distraction-free reading experience.

### 1. Information Collection and Use

**I do not collect, store, or share any personal data.**

The App operates entirely locally on your device for its core reading functionalities. It does not require you to create an account, does not track your behavior, and does not harvest any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or location.

### 2. Network Access and Downloaded Content

The App requires internet access strictly for the following functional purposes:
- **Audio Recitations:** To stream or download Quranic audio files (recitations) so you can listen offline later.
- **Tafsir/Translations:** To fetch Tafsir or translation databases if requested by the user.
- **In-App Updates:** To occasionally check a configuration file or the Google Play Store to notify you of critical app updates.

None of your reading habits or download histories are transmitted to me. Downloaded media files are stored locally on your device.

### 3. Advertising and Analytics

Tabattal is **100% Ad-Free**. I do not use third-party advertising networks, nor do I integrate third-party analytics SDKs (such as Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel) that track your usage.

### 4. Children’s Privacy

Since my App does not collect any personal information, it is completely safe for children of all ages. I comply with the requirements of COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar global regulations.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update my Privacy Policy from time to time. If I make any changes, I will post the updated Privacy Policy in the App's repository. Your continued use of the App after the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of such changes.
