Everything You Need (And More)

We didn't cut any corners. Shrinkeo is packed with enterprise-grade features wrapped in an elegant UI.

Hardware & CPU Encoding

Stick to the highly optimized Software (CPU) default for maximum quality, or manually harness your hardware by selecting NVIDIA (NVENC), AMD (AMF), or Intel (QSV) for blazing-fast compression.

Advanced Video Control

Tune quality via a professional CRF slider, downscale 4K resolutions, cap framerates, and choose H.264 or H.265 to export to MP4, MKV, or MOV.

100% Offline & Private

Your videos never leave your computer. No cloud uploads, no privacy risks, no watermarks, and absolutely zero file size limits.

Smart Audio Management

Keep the original audio pristine, re-encode it to AAC to save space, or strip it out entirely for silent videos.

Batch Queue & Context Menu

Drag and drop 100+ videos to process them sequentially, or instantly compress any videos using the "Compress with Shrinkeo" Windows Right-Click Menu.

Satisfying Analytics

Watch live progress, get accurate ETAs, compare original vs. compressed file sizes, and track your total Global Saved Space over time!

A unified, modern Experience

Everything is accessible through a clean, modern interface designed for maximum productivity and simplicity.
